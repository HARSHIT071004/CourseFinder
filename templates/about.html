About CourseFinder

A content-based course recommendation system powered by NLP.

The Problem

With tens of thousands of courses available, finding the right one is overwhelming. Search results are often noisy, and platforms rarely help you discover courses that match what you already enjoy.

Our Approach

CourseFinder uses content-based filtering. It analyzes course attributes, converts them into TF-IDF feature vectors, and computes cosine similarity between every pair. The result: precise, data-driven recommendations.

The Stack

  • Flask — lightweight Python web framework
  • scikit-learn — TfidfVectorizer and cosine_similarity
  • pandas — data loading and manipulation
  • Vanilla JS — no framework overhead

NLP Explained

TF-IDF measures how important a word is to a course relative to the entire catalog. Rare, specific terms score higher than common words. Cosine similarity then measures how close two courses are in this vector space.
